Introduction: Color Theory

Color Theory

As an artist it is essential for me to know color theory. During my years of making art I always have a difficult time applying colors in my work, so I have only draw in black and white. However, learning color theory is very important as colors will further enhance your artwork. Color theory is a study of how colors works together and the message it convey to us. It is used by all artists, designers and creaters to convey emotions and messages of their work. The traditional color theory uses the color wheel which comprises the primary, secondary and tertiary colors. This theory was developed by Isaac Newton in 1666 when he invented the color wheel, which is still used today. Newton saw that colors are human perception not only just qualities of light. Still colors are created using light, which are created by certain wavelengths and frequency, that is where visible light comes in. Below is the visible light spectrum.
