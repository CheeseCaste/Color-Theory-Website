Importance of Color Theory

Color theory is the first thing we should know when using colors in our works. Color theory has long been the guide and basis on how we apply colors to express our messages to other people. Serving its vital role for creative expression in different fields of art, color theory is not just a lesson in arts, an academic requirement but also plays a role in improving and showing your expression in arts.
