How to color with monochromatic colors

Here’s a step-by-step guide using blue as the base color for monochromatic colors:

Step 1: Base Color

First, pick a main color that matches the mood or theme of your design. For example, you could choose blue if you want a calm vibe, or red if you’re going for something more energetic and bold.

Step 2: Mix light and dark colors with base color

Next, play around with lighter and darker versions of that color to add some contrast. So, if you’re using blue, you can mix in light blue, navy blue, and maybe a medium blue to keep things interesting.

Step 3: Add variations

Finally, spread those color variations across your design. Use the lighter shades for backgrounds, the darker ones for text or accents, and make sure everything feels balanced.

A monochromatic color scheme is a great way to make your design look clean and consistent without being too flashy or overwhelming.
