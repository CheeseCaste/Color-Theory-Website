Lesson 7: Triadic colors

Pick a color

Triadic color scheme involves using three colors that are evenly spaced around the color wheel. This is a form of triangle in the color wheel in short. Using these three (orange, green, and violet) colors together can create a dynamic and visually appealing design, as they all enhance each other's intensity and vibrancy.

The variety of objects makes it suitable for triadic. Having a different colors makes the object distinguid them from other, as well as giving the mood of the work lively.
