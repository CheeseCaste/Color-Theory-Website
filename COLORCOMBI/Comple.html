Lesson 5: Complementary colors

Pick a color

or

Complementary colors are pairs of colors that are directly opposite one another on the color wheel are known as complementary colors. They produce a striking contrast and give each other a more lively appearance when positioned close to one another. Just think of how uninteresting the artwork would be if I used the same hue for the sun as well.

Adding a complement color is a simple way to enhance the whole artwork. The dominance of the purple made it too full, however after adding yellow in it, the whole work became balance and even added a story and mood in it. The constrast between the sun and the background made as if the sun was the symbol for answer in a surrounding full of mystery.
